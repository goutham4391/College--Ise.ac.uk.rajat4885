LSE Discovery Sessions

Our LSE Discovery Sessions will introduce prospective students to the range of study options at LSE, student life and the application process. The sessions will involve a short introductory presentation delivered by a member of the Student Marketing, Recruitment and Study Abroad team, followed by the opportunity for attendees to submit their own questions.

Discovery Sessions will take place via Zoom.

Advance booking is required. Please select your chosen date from the list below to book a place. 

Event times are UK local time.

LSE Discovery Sessions – Undergraduate

  • Wednesday, 2 November 2022, 10-11am
  • Wednesday, 30 November 2022, 4-5pm

LSE Discovery Sessions - Graduate 

  • Wednesday, 2 November 2022, 4-5pm
  • Wednesday, 16 November 2022, 10-11am (part of Virtual Graduate Open Events)
  • Wednesday, 23 November 2022, 4-5pm (part of Virtual Graduate Open Events)
